**2. Download Satoshi font files:**
Download Satoshi-Regular.woff2, Satoshi-Medium.woff2, Satoshi-Bold.woff2, and Satoshi-Black.woff2 from Fontshare (https://api.fontshare.com/v2/css?f[]=satoshi@400,500,700,900&display=swap). **3. Create `apps/web/src/lib/fonts.ts`:**
