UploadKit offers four subscription tiers. All plans include access to the full SDK and dashboard — tiers differ in storage limits, bandwidth, upload count, and project/key capacity.

## Subscription tiers

| Feature | Free | Pro | Team | Enterprise |
|---------|------|-----|------|-----------|
| **Price** | $0/mo | $15/mo | $35/mo | Custom |
| **Storage** | 5 GB | 100 GB | 1 TB | Unlimited |
| **Bandwidth** | 2 GB/mo | 200 GB/mo | 2 TB/mo | Unlimited |
| **Max file size** | 4 MB | 512 MB | 5 GB | 10 GB |
| **Uploads/month** | 1,000 | 50,000 | 500,000 | Unlimited |
| **Projects** | 2 | 10 | 50 | Unlimited |
| **API keys** | 2 | 10 | 50 | Unlimited |
| **Overage billing** | — | Yes | Yes | Negotiated |

<Callout type="info">
  Free tier accounts do not have overage billing — uploads are blocked once the monthly quota is reached. Upgrade to Pro to enable overages.
</Callout>

## Overage pricing

Pro and Team plans continue to work after quota is reached by charging for overage usage at the end of the billing period:

| Resource | Overage rate |
|---------|-------------|
| Storage | $0.02 per GB |
| Bandwidth | $0.01 per GB |
| Uploads | $0.001 per upload |

Overages are billed via Stripe at the end of each calendar month. You can monitor usage in real time from the **Usage** page to avoid unexpected charges.

## Upgrading your plan

1. Navigate to **Billing** in the sidebar
2. Click **Upgrade** next to the plan you want
3. You are redirected to Stripe Checkout
4. Enter your payment details and complete the subscription
5. Your account is upgraded immediately after payment

Upgrade takes effect instantly — your new limits apply as soon as the Stripe Checkout session completes.

## Managing your subscription

To change your plan, update payment information, or view invoice history:

1. Navigate to **Billing** in the sidebar
2. Click **Manage subscription**
3. You are redirected to the Stripe Billing Portal

From the Billing Portal you can:

- **Upgrade or downgrade** your plan
- **Update payment method** (credit card or bank account)
- **Download invoices** (PDF)
- **Cancel your subscription** (you keep access until the end of the billing period)

## Canceling your subscription

Cancel from the Stripe Billing Portal. After cancellation:

- You retain access to your current plan until the end of the billing period
- At the end of the period, your account reverts to the Free tier
- Files exceeding the Free tier 5 GB limit are not automatically deleted — you have 30 days to remove excess files before they are flagged

## Usage monitoring

The **Usage** page shows real-time usage for the current billing period:

- **Storage** — current storage used vs. your plan limit
- **Bandwidth** — egress from CDN this month
- **Uploads** — number of successful uploads this month

Usage updates within a few minutes of each upload or deletion.

<Callout type="info">
  Usage is tracked per **account**, not per project. Your 5 GB Free tier storage is shared across all projects in your account.
</Callout>
